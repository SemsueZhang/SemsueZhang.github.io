电路习题选做

Posted on 2026-03-09 In Exercises

课程编码：DCS2026. 课程名称：电路理论基础 （一）. 所选教材：电路基础（原书第6版），机械工业出版社，2018.9；ISBN 978-7-111-61076-2. 授课学期：26 春季

这学期选的专选课，内容偏物理电路（和模电还不太一样）那一块的内容. 本文主要为作业题的记录.

Introsuction to the Course(original text): “Fundamentals of Circuit Theory” is an important foundational course for undergraduate students in various computer-related majors. Its main content covers the fundamental theories and methods of time-invariant lumped-parameter circuits. Through this course, students will master the basic concepts and principles of circuit analysis, develop their ability to analyze and calculate circuits, and lay a foundation for subsequent circuit-related courses.

By studying this course, students will understand and master the fundamental theories and analytical methods of linear, time-invariant circuits. This includes: 1) Kirchhoff’s laws and the voltage-current relationships of various components; 2) understanding and mastering the power and energy relationships of various circuit elements; 3) skillfully applying various methods and laws to analyze and calculate circuits, and mastering the concepts of equivalence and matching; 4) the characteristics of first-order and second-order circuits; 5) analyzing sinusoidal steady-state circuits using the phasor method.
